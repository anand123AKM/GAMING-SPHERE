Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic game played between two players who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid with X or O. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

Stone Paper Scissor

The Stone-Paper-Scissors game, also known as Rock-Paper-Scissors, is a simple hand game usually played between two people. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand: rock, paper or scissors . The game has three possible outcomes other than a tie: rock crushes scissors, scissors cuts paper, or paper covers rock.

Snake Game

The Snake game is a classic video game concept where the player maneuvers the snake which grows in length with each point, the snake eats. The game ends when the snake either moves off the screen or moves into itself. The objective of the game is to eat as many as possible and thus make the snake grow as long as possible.
